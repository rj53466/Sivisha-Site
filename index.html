Memorandum of Association

Core objects of Sivisha Industries

  1. To carry on the business of retail pharmacies for sale and distribution of medicines, medical devices, and healthcare products.
  2. To establish and operate pharmaceutical manufacturing plants for drugs, formulations, and allied products.
  3. To manufacture and retail cosmetic and personal care products through dedicated plants and outlets.
  4. To establish and manage hospitals, clinics, diagnostic and healthcare service facilities.
  5. To undertake related research, development, import, export, and other allied activities supporting the above businesses.
